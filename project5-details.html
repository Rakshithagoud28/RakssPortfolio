AI That Listens, Understands, and Responds

NexaTalk is an AI-powered voice assistant designed to provide seamless, hands-free interaction. Users can speak naturally, and NexaTalk understands their queries, offering intelligent and concise responses. The system combines advanced speech recognition, natural language processing, and text-to-speech technologies to create an interactive conversational experience.

The assistant uses OpenAI’s Whisper model to transcribe the user’s spoken words into text accurately. Once the speech is converted, the text is sent to the Groq API, a large language model, which generates intelligent responses based on the user’s query. These responses are then converted back into audio using pyttsx3, allowing the assistant to speak naturally to the user.

NexaTalk offers a real-time conversation interface built with Streamlit, showing both the user’s input and AI responses on the screen. It includes stop-word detection, so the conversation can be ended gracefully with words like “Quit” or “Stop.” Temporary audio recordings are managed efficiently, ensuring smooth processing without storing unnecessary data. The app is interactive, intuitive, and optimized for a fluid voice-driven experience.

NexaTalk can be used as a personal assistant, a learning tool, or a hands-free AI companion for day-to-day tasks. Its integration of speech recognition, AI-driven conversation, and text-to-speech makes it ideal for users who prefer voice interactions over typing. By combining multiple modern technologies, NexaTalk bridges the gap between human speech and intelligent AI responses, delivering a natural and engaging conversational experience.
